Welcome to LSF-Mobile!

Tap the folder names in the list below to expand them and browse all available courses. If you find a course that you are interested in, tap it to show additional information about it. Add it to your timetable by clicking the 'Add to timetable' - Button in the course description. You can switch between your timetable and the course-list by tapping the respective buttons on top of the list. If you want to change or delete an entry in your timetable, just tap it! A menu opens up and you can make changes from there.
